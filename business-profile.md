# Business Profile — Manpro

> The **instructor sample** business profile — the worked example students follow. Notice it's just a
> plain-language **description** (plus branding). It does **not** define classes — those get derived from
> this description in Module 2. (Each student writes their own version of this for their chosen business.)

---

## The business

- **Name:** Manpro
- **What it does / specialty:** An auto repair shop that specializes in vehicle **maintenance**.
- **Tagline:** "Maintenance done right."
- **Who its customers are:** Local car owners who want dependable, on-time upkeep for their vehicles.

## What Manpro does (in plain words)

Customers bring their cars to Manpro for maintenance. For each visit we write up a repair job — what
needs doing, how urgent it is, and its status from drop-off to done — and we note which mechanic handles
it. We keep track of our customers and the vehicles they own, so we know whose car is whose and what's
been done.

## Branding (visual identity)

- **Logo:** 🔧
- **Colors:** deep green `#15795f` + dark slate `#0f172a`
- **Font feel:** clean, modern sans-serif
- **Vibe:** trustworthy, professional, no-nonsense
